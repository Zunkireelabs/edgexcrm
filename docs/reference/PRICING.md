## Tier positioning

### 🌱 Starter — $39 / month
**For:** A solo founder, freelance counselor, or small office of 1–3 people who need to stop tracking leads in spreadsheets.

You get one form, one pipeline, and enough capacity to manage your first few hundred leads with your small team. Everything you need to look professional and never lose a lead.

**Stops being right when:** you outgrow 3 seats, need API access, or want AI-assisted lead prioritization.

### 🚀 Professional — $129 / month
**For:** A growing 5–15 person team running structured sales: education consultancies, real-estate brokers, recruitment agencies, B2B service companies.

This is the workhorse tier. You get multi-pipeline support (e.g., one for new applications, one for renewals), AI lead scoring to focus your team on hot leads, full API + webhook access for Zapier and custom integrations, and the Counselor role to scope what each rep sees.

**Stops being right when:** you operate multiple brands, need white-label, or scale past 15 reps.

### 🏢 Enterprise — $399 / month
**For:** Agencies serving multiple clients, multi-brand organizations, or any team beyond 15 reps that needs full white-label control.

Unlimited everything — seats, leads, pipelines, webhooks. Premium support with a dedicated success manager, a 99.9% uptime SLA, and (rolling out 2026) full white-label control including custom domains for both forms and the dashboard.

## FAQ

**Is there a free trial?**
Yes — 14 days on any tier, no credit card required. Full access to all features.

**Can I change plans?**
Yes. Upgrades are immediate and prorated. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

**What happens if I exceed my lead limit?**
We don't lock you out. New leads continue to flow in, but you'll see an in-app notice prompting an upgrade. After 30 days over-limit, additional leads queue until you upgrade or add an overage pack.

**Do unused seats refund?**
We bill flat per tier, so unused seats aren't a thing on Starter or Professional. Enterprise is custom-quoted.

**Can I self-host or get a private instance?**
Enterprise customers can request a single-tenant deployment. Contact sales for pricing.

**Is my data isolated from other tenants?**
Yes. Every row is enforced via PostgreSQL Row-Level Security — your data is never queryable by another tenant. Audited via automated tests on every release.
