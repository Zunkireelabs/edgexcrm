## How to read this

Every feature is classified as one of:
- **Global** ÔÇö used by every tenant regardless of industry. Lives outside `src/industries/`.
- **Industry-aware** ÔÇö used by every tenant but adapts labels/behavior per industry.
- **Industry-scoped** ÔÇö only available to tenants in the listed industries. Lives in `src/industries/<id>/features/`.
- **Shared** ÔÇö used by multiple industries but not all. Lives in `src/industries/_shared/features/`.

When adding or moving features, update this table and the constants in `src/industries/_registry.ts`. CLAUDE.md ┬º Industry Scoping Rules has the decision tree.
